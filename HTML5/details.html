How to beat the boss...spoiler alert!

Just aim to the red spots near his eyes

Keep shooting at these spots until the eyes open, then hit quickly both eyes with your laser beam.

Bonus and spoiler no 2: get a new weapon by cutting the tail off of the boss

Before finishing him, try to cut his tail, you will get a new weapon

Just try to stay behind him, hitting his tail with your melee weapon, after a few hits the tail will fall and you will get a new bonus weapon, then finisht the boss.
